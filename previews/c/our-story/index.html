Our Story

Since 2009, Walden Ridge has executed renovation programs for select-service and upscale hotel assets across repeat ownership platforms. Formerly known as HRS Group, the platform has delivered 60+ branded renovations and six ground-up developments.

Timeline
2009
Entered the hotel sector and formed the renovation operating platform.
15+ Years
HRS Group operating history carried forward into the Walden Ridge platform.
Today
Repeat renovation programs across seven flag families and multi-asset owners.

The company was formed to bring disciplined, repeatable execution to brand-mandated capital cycles, work defined by PIP requirements, fixed inspection windows, and occupied-asset constraints.

Over 15+ years, Walden Ridge built an integrated platform that keeps procurement, millwork, FF&E, and field coordination under a single operating rhythm, reducing handoffs and schedule variance.

Today the team works repeatedly with multi-asset owners across Hilton, Marriott, Hyatt, IHG, Wyndham, La Quinta, and Extended Stay flag families, typically delivering full-scope renovations in 4-6 month windows.

Based in Chattanooga, TN, Walden Ridge partners with institutional and owner-operator clients who value predictable outcomes, compliance certainty, and disciplined capital planning.
