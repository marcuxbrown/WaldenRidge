Our Story

60+ renovations · 6 ground-up developments · 7 flag families

Nathan Moore founded Walden Ridge in 2009 to bring trade-level execution discipline to branded hotel renovations.

Before entering the hospitality sector, Nathan spent six years leading residential renovation and new-construction projects. Hotel capital programs required the same discipline: predictable schedules, in-house millwork, and direct field oversight

The firm was structured to address the handoffs and schedule risk common in traditional general-contractor models. Walden Ridge integrated procurement, millwork production, FF&E coordination, and field supervision under single ownership, allowing the company to deliver brand-mandated PIPs within fixed inspection windows while properties remained occupied

Since 2009, the firm has completed 60+ branded renovations and six ground-up developments across Hilton, Marriott, Hyatt, IHG, Wyndham, La Quinta, and Extended Stay, working repeatedly with multi-asset owners who value schedule certainty, cost discipline, and first-attempt brand compliance

Walden Ridge remains founder-led, maintaining the operational discipline and hands-on execution that have defined the work from the beginning
