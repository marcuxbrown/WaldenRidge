Our Story

Walden Ridge executes renovation programs for select-service and upscale hotel assets. Operating since 2010, the company works repeatedly with multi-asset owners across Hilton, Marriott, IHG, and Hyatt flag families.

In branded hospitality, renovation timing is scheduled by the system, not by impulse. The work repeats in known cycles, with shorter execution windows that keep owners, contractors, and capital partners aligned on predictable outcomes.
