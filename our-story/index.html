Our Story

60+ renovations · 6 ground-up developments · 7 flag families

Walden Ridge has executed renovation programs for branded hotel assets across repeat ownership groups, delivering 60+ renovations and six ground-up developments.

The company was formed to bring repeatable execution to brand-mandated capital cycles defined by PIP requirements, fixed inspection windows, and occupied-asset constraints

Walden Ridge built an integrated operation that keeps procurement, millwork, FF&E, and field coordination under a single operating rhythm, reducing handoffs and schedule risk

Today the team works repeatedly with multi-asset owners across Hilton, Marriott, Hyatt, IHG, Wyndham, La Quinta, and Extended Stay flag families, typically delivering full-scope renovations in 4-6 month windows

Based in Chattanooga, TN, Walden Ridge partners with owners and owner-operators who value schedule certainty, cost discipline, and compliance readiness
